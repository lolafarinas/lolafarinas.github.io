About me

My Scientific Journey

As a physicist and researcher, my career is a testament to the power of curiosity and interdisciplinary collaboration. My journey began with a Ph.D. in Physics from the Technical University of Valencia, where I first learned to **uncover the hidden connections between seemingly unrelated concepts**.

I am a strong believer in research with real-world impact. This passion led me to specialize in **Non-Destructive Testing**, where I apply advanced ultrasonic and acoustic technologies to solve practical challenges. Whether it's evaluating the quality of crops or assessing the health of battery cells, my work bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and tangible, real-world applications.

My work is not just about data and equations; it's about connecting with people. I am passionate about **science outreach** and sharing the excitement of discovery with the public. Through every project, I seek to inspire the next generation of scientists and show how fundamental research can lead to groundbreaking innovations.

Beyond the Lab

My journey is driven by a profound belief that science should be a force for positive change. Each project, collaboration, and discovery is a step toward building a better, more sustainable future. My vision is to continue bridging diverse fields and inspire others to see the world not just as it is, but as it could be.
